# Ledgr Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** May 31, 2026
**Effective Date:** June 1, 2026

## 1. Introduction

Ledgr FZ-LLC ("Ledgr," "we," "us," "our") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we collect, use, and safeguard your data. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices.

**Scope:** This policy applies to all data collected through ledgr.ai and related services (web, mobile apps, APIs, integrations). It does not apply to third-party services linked from Ledgr (QuickBooks, Xero, Plaid, etc.).

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide

**Account Registration:**
- Name, email, phone number, job title
- Business name, registration number, VAT ID
- Address (business and billing)
- Payment information (credit card, bank details)

**Service Usage:**
- Invoices, expenses, receipts, and financial documents
- Bank account information (via Plaid integration)
- Employee payroll data
- Tax returns and compliance documents
- Customer and vendor information

**Communications:**
- Emails, chat messages, and support tickets
- Feedback, surveys, and feature requests

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

**Usage Data:**
- IP address, browser type, device type, OS
- Pages visited, time spent, features used
- Login/logout times, frequency of access
- Error messages and debugging data

**Cookies & Tracking:**
- Session cookies (required for login)
- Analytics cookies (Google Analytics 4)
- No third-party tracking or retargeting cookies

**Integrations:**
- Data from QuickBooks, Xero, Plaid APIs (with your authorization)
- Bank transaction metadata (automated classification)

### 2.3 Information from Third Parties

- Bank and financial institutions (payment processing)
- Third-party identity verification services
- FTA (Federal Tax Authority) public registries (for compliance checking)

## 3. How We Use Your Information

### 3.1 Service Delivery
We use your data to:
- Provide, maintain, and improve the Service
- Process transactions and payments
- Send invoices, receipts, and billing statements
- Provide customer support (email, WhatsApp, in-app chat)
- Execute integrations with third-party software

### 3.2 Compliance & Legal Obligations
We use your data to:
- Verify compliance with UAE tax and labor laws
- Generate FTA e-invoicing and VAT reports
- Respond to legal requests and regulatory inquiries
- Detect and prevent fraud or unauthorized access
- Maintain audit logs and security records

### 3.3 Service Improvement
We use your data (in anonymized/aggregated form) to:
- Analyze usage patterns and feature adoption
- Identify bugs and performance issues
- Develop new features and functionality
- Train AI/ML models (e.g., invoice OCR, tax calculations)

**Opt-out:** You may decline non-essential analytics by adjusting settings (see Section 8).

### 3.4 Communication
We use your email and phone to send:
- Account notifications (login, password reset, payment reminders)
- Service updates and maintenance notifications (48 hours' notice minimum)
- Feature announcements and product improvements (quarterly maximum)
- Security alerts (unauthorized access, suspicious activity)

You may opt out of non-essential communications via account settings.

### 3.5 Marketing (With Consent)
If you opt in, we may send:
- Monthly insights and compliance tips
- Webinar and event invitations
- Product news and case studies

We do NOT sell or rent your data to third parties for marketing purposes.

## 4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data under the following legal bases:

| Data Type | Purpose | Legal Basis |

| Account & billing info | Service delivery | Contract performance |

## 5. Data Retention

We retain data as follows:

| Data Type | Retention Period | Reason |
|-----------|-----------------|--------|
| Account info | Duration + 3 years after deletion | Tax audit requirements |
| Financial records | 7 years | UAE accounting law |
| Payment records | 7 years | Financial compliance |
| Email/chat logs | 3 years | Legal/support history |
| Usage analytics | 2 years | Service improvement |
| Backups | 30 days after deletion | Disaster recovery |

After retention periods, data is securely deleted or anonymized.

## 6. Data Sharing & Transfers

### 6.1 Who We Share Data With

**Third parties we share data with (as required by law or contract):**

- **FTA (Federal Tax Authority)** – Tax filings, e-invoicing records (mandatory)
- **Banks & Payment Processors** – Transaction verification, fraud prevention
- **Plaid Inc.** – Bank account verification (with your authorization)
- **QuickBooks/Xero** – Data sync (with your authorization)
- **Hosting & Infrastructure** – AWS (data storage, backups)
- **Email Service Provider** – SendGrid (for account notifications)
- **Analytics Provider** – Google Analytics 4 (aggregated usage data only)
- **Legal Representatives** – In response to lawful requests or disputes

**We do NOT share:**
- Personal financial data with competitors
- Your data with advertisers or marketers
- Invoices or tax documents with unauthorized third parties

### 6.2 International Data Transfers

**Data Residency:** All data is stored in AWS infrastructure within the UAE (me-south-1 region) in compliance with FTA data residency requirements.

**Exceptions:**
- Plaid Inc. (US-based): Bank data transferred for verification per their Privacy Policy
- Google Analytics: Aggregated analytics routed through Google servers (no PII)

You consent to these transfers by using Ledgr.

## 7. Data Security

### 7.1 Security Measures

Ledgr implements industry-standard security controls:

- **Encryption in Transit:** TLS 1.3 (256-bit) for all data transmission
- **Encryption at Rest:** AES-256 encryption for all stored data
- **Access Controls:** Role-based access; multi-factor authentication (MFA) available
- **Network Security:** WAF (Web Application Firewall), DDoS protection
- **Data Centers:** ISO 27001-certified AWS facilities in UAE
- **Monitoring:** 24/7 intrusion detection and incident response
- **Backups:** Automated daily backups with 30-day retention

### 7.2 Your Responsibilities

You are responsible for:
- Keeping login credentials confidential
- Enabling MFA on your account
- Reporting unauthorized access immediately
- Maintaining secure devices and internet connections

### 7.3 Data Breach Notification

In the event of unauthorized access, Ledgr will notify affected users within 24 hours and provide guidance on protective measures. Ledgr is not liable for breaches due to user negligence.

### 8.2 Correction & Deletion
You have the right to:

- Request deletion of non-essential data (within 30 days)

### 8.3 Opt-Out Rights
You may opt out of:

- Access your data

- Object to automated decision-making
- Lodge a complaint with the Information Security Authority
