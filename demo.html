Common Questions About the Demo

Is this a real live system or a simulation? +

This is a fully functional demo running the same codebase as the live platform. All data is anonymized and randomized for privacy. The agents, reports, and calculations are real—you're seeing actual AI decision-making in action.

Can I edit transactions or change data in the demo? +

This demo is read-only to preserve the integrity of the sample data. When you get early access, you'll have full edit permissions, undo capability, and all the collaboration features you'd expect from a modern platform.

What happens when I upgrade from the demo? +

When you start with Ledgr, we'll help you import your existing data, configure your entity structure, connect your bank accounts, and set up integration with any existing tools. Your onboarding is hands-on—your assigned accountant will walk you through everything.

How long does it take to set up my own account? +

Setup typically takes 5-7 business days for small to mid-market firms. We handle the technical side (integrations, entity configuration, compliance setup). You focus on providing your historical data and connecting your banking. Your accountant coordinates everything.
