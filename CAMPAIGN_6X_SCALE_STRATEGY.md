# Ledgr 6x Campaign Scale Strategy
## Multi-Variation Campaign for Trial User Acquisition & Product Validation

**Last Updated:** May 31, 2026  
**Campaign Owner:** Ledgr Trial Program  
**Timeline:** June 1 - September 30, 2026  
**Target:** 6 strategic campaign angles × 4 content variations = 24 total assets

## Campaign Overview

This campaign uses **6 distinct positioning angles** to reach different trial user segments. Each angle generates:
- 2x AI-generated images (Higgsfield)
- 1x Short-form video (Higgsfield)
- 1x Copy variation (Claude + Higgsfield prompt)

**Total Production:** 6 themes × 4 assets = 24 marketing assets from single execution

## Asset Generation Workflow

### Phase 1: Prompt Engineering (Claude)
For each campaign angle, generate Higgsfield prompts:

```
Campaign Angle: Speed & Automation
Claude generates:
- Image 1 prompt: "Modern SaaS dashboard showing VAT calculations updating in real-time, 
  clean Corgi orange (#FF5C00) accent, light gray background, professional UI, 
  motion lines suggesting speed, high-quality 4K photography style"
  
- Image 2 prompt: "Split-screen before/after: left shows accountant drowning in spreadsheets,
  right shows relaxed founder with Ledgr dashboard, Corgi design system, professional, 
  high-contrast, clean typography"
  
- Video prompt: "Screen recording of Ledgr dashboard live-updating VAT line items, 
  smooth 60fps transitions, Corgi orange highlights, uplifting background music, 
  text overlay: 'VAT tracking. Happening right now.', 3-5 second duration"
  
- Copy prompt: "Write a 280-character tweet about VAT automation that speaks to busy founders.
  Tone: confident, direct, no jargon. Highlight the time-saving aspect. Include a CTA."
```

### Phase 2: Asset Generation (Higgsfield)
Execute generation for each asset:

```
Image 1: nano_banana_pro or seedream_v5_lite
  → Ultra-high quality, 4K, photorealistic
  → Corgi brand colors (#FF5C00, #f9f9f9, #191919)
  → Professional product photography aesthetic

Image 2: nano_banana_pro or gpt_image_2
  → Comparison/contrast visual
  → Infographic-style clarity
  → Same color system

Video: kling3_0 or cinema_studio_3_0
  → 5-15 second duration
  → 1080p+ resolution
  → Smooth transitions
  → Optional: voiceover or music bed

Copy: Claude (direct, no generation needed)
  → 280 characters (Twitter-optimized)
  → 150 characters (LinkedIn headline)
  → 50-word paragraph (Instagram caption)
```

### Phase 3: Batch Deployment
Push all 24 assets to:
1. **Social Media:** LinkedIn (6 posts), Twitter/X (6 threads), Instagram (6 carousels)
2. **Email:** 6-part waitlist nurture sequence
3. **Ads:** Google Ads, Meta ads (A/B test all 6 angles)
4. **Blog:** 6 supporting blog posts with embedded assets

## Notes & Risk Mitigation

**Low Risk:**
- Asset generation is parallelizable (can run all 24 simultaneously)
- Angles are independent (one angle underperforming won't kill others)
- GA4 tracking provides real-time visibility

**Medium Risk:**
- Higgsfield quality consistency across 24 assets (mitigate: QA review all)
- Budget allocation (mitigate: start with equal split, shift based on early results)
- Message-market fit (mitigate: test early, iterate quickly)

**High Risk:**
- No direct feedback loop from ads back to product roadmap (mitigate: assign someone to monitor feedback + tag issues)
