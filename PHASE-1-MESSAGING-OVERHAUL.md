## 8️⃣ COPY GUIDELINES (Brand Voice)

### Tone
- **Confident but not arrogant**
- **Clear, not jargony**
- **Action-oriented, not passive**
- **Precise, not vague**

### Language Dos
- ✅ "Complete control"
- ✅ "Real-time visibility"
- ✅ "Your decision"
- ✅ "Rock-solid"
- ✅ "Zero downtime"

### Language Don'ts
- ❌ "AI-powered" (overused, vague)
