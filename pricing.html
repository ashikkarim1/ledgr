Pricing FAQ

The questions that come up before signing.

Is the Free tier really free?

Yes. No card, no time limit. Most freelancers and very early businesses stay on Free for as long as it fits — we make money when you outgrow it, not before. The 50-invoice / 10-employee caps reset every calendar month.

What does "founder pricing" mean for the launch cohort?

The first 80 paying customers lock in the prices you see on this page for 24 months. After that you stay on whatever pricing you signed up at, never higher — even if the public price goes up.

I have multiple legal entities. How does that work?

Each plan includes one to five entities — see the matrix. Additional entities are AED 199 / mo for Solo, AED 449 / mo for Growth, included on Scale. We bill them on one invoice and you can run consolidations across them on Scale.

What if I exceed the transaction or employee cap?

We never auto-upgrade you. You'll get a heads-up at 80% of the cap and a recommendation, then any overage in that month is billed at AED 0.50 / transaction or AED 25 / extra employee. You decide whether to keep going or move to the next plan.

Can I move down a plan if I shrink?

Yes, at any month-end. We'll migrate your data and update the limits — you never lose history, just the higher-tier features stop being available.

How does Ledgr compare to my current bookkeeping firm on price?

A typical UAE SME pays AED 4,000–6,000 / mo to an outsourced firm and still runs payroll themselves. Ledgr Growth (AED 1,499) replaces that and the firm's deliverables, with a dedicated chartered accountant included. Run the numbers on our free FTA readiness check — the report includes a side-by-side cost comparison for your specific shape.

How do payments work?

Monthly via card (Stripe) or annually via bank transfer (Wio, ENBD, Mashreq accepted). Annual prepay saves 10%. Invoices and payment receipts are FTA-compliant and Peppol-issued, naturally.

What happens if I leave?

You export your transactions, ledgers, source documents and audit trail in CSV, JSON and PDF. We help you migrate to any competitor — no clawbacks, no hostage data. Your seven-year statutory archive is downloadable in one zip.
