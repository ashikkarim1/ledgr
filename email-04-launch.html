Hello,

After months of development, refinement, and testing with leading accounting firms, we're thrilled to announce: Ledgr is now available.

We built Ledgr to solve a real problem: accounting firms are stuck between two bad choices. They can hire more junior staff to handle growing transaction volumes (expensive, quality variable, burnout risk). Or they can try to automate everything and strip out judgment (alienates experienced staff, produces errors).

Ledgr offers a third way. AI handles transaction categorization, matching, and validation. Your team handles judgment, interpretation, and strategy. Result: faster close cycles, fewer errors, better client service.

What's Included

  • Real-time Transaction Processing: Transactions are categorized and validated continuously, not in a month-end rush.
  • Automated VAT Compliance: Monthly VAT returns are prepared automatically. Your team reviews and files in days, not weeks.
  • Comprehensive Audit Trail: Every decision is logged and documented. Audit readiness is built in.
  • Client Visibility Portal: Clients see their financial position in real-time. No more waiting for month-end reports.
  • Scalable Architecture: Add clients without proportionally increasing headcount or close cycle time.
