# LEDGR: UAE FinTech for SME Accounting & Compliance

## One-Page Executive Summary

**Ledgr** is a cloud-based accounting platform purpose-built for UAE SMEs, delivering real-time compliance with FTA e-invoicing, VAT calculations, corporate tax, and payroll integration—backed by AI agents that automate invoice processing, bank reconciliation, and regulatory reporting.

### Market Opportunity

- **TAM:** 600,000+ registered SMEs in UAE; 85% using outdated legacy software or spreadsheets
- **Growth Trigger:** FTA e-invoicing mandate (Phase 1: Jan 2027 for AED 50M+ revenue; Phase 2: July 2027 for all SMEs) creates urgent compliance requirement
- **Competitive Gap:** Existing solutions (Xero, QuickBooks, Zoho, Wafeq) lack native UAE-specific regulatory automation; require expensive consulting or manual compliance
- **Market Size:** USD 180M+ annual software spend by UAE SMEs; growing 18-22% CAGR

### Product Differentiation

1. **Native FTA Compliance** – Real-time e-invoicing (Peppol) with zero configuration
2. **AI-Powered Automation** – Invoice OCR, bank reconciliation, tax calculation, payroll compliance (MOHRE, WPS integration)
3. **Regulatory Intelligence** – Automated updates for UAE corporate tax, VAT, labor law changes
4. **Local Support** – Arabic-language UI, localized documentation, 24/7 email/WhatsApp support
5. **Integration Ecosystem** – QuickBooks, Xero, Plaid, WhatsApp, Stripe, Azure OpenAI, AWS infrastructure

### Traction & Readiness

- **MVP Complete:** 13-page responsive platform with full feature set (accounting module, pricing module, calculator, document templates, accountant directory, AI agent integration)
- **Pre-launch:** 200+ waitlist signups; partnerships pending with UAE accounting associations and chambers of commerce
- **Tech Stack:** Static HTML/CSS/JS frontend (fast, secure); scalable TypeScript backend; Postgres + Redis; deployed on Vercel (99.95% uptime, auto-HTTPS, global CDN)
- **Regulatory Approval:** Fully compliant with FTA Phase 1 & 2 requirements; legal review complete
