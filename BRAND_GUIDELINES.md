## 1. Brand Story

**Ledgr** is building the future of accounting for UAE SMEs. We believe compliance shouldn't be a burden—it should be invisible, instant, and built into every transaction.

For 20+ years, SMEs have relied on spreadsheets, offshore accountants, and outdated software that doesn't understand UAE's unique regulatory landscape. The FTA e-invoicing mandate (Jan 2027) is forcing a reckoning: adapt or face fines.

Ledgr eliminates that risk. With real-time FTA compliance, AI-powered automation, and local expertise, we're becoming the default fintech platform for UAE business.

**Why Ledgr?**
- **Modern:** Built on cloud-native architecture (not legacy software)
- **Local:** Native UAE compliance, Arabic UI, 24/7 local support
- **Intelligent:** AI agents automate 80+ hours/month of compliance work
- **Trusted:** Designed by accountants, built for SMEs

## 2. Visual Identity

### 2.1 Color Palette

**Primary Brand Color**
- **Orange (Brand Primary):** `#FF5C00`
  - Usage: CTA buttons, active states, primary UI elements, brand mark
  - Psychology: Modern, bold, trustworthy (fintech standard)

**Semantic Colors**
- **Success (Green):** `#34c759` – Payments approved, compliance met, positive actions
- **Error (Red):** `#ff405d` – Warnings, overdue payments, non-compliance
- **Warning (Amber):** `#ff9500` – Caution, review needed, deadlines approaching
- **Info (Blue):** `#0A84FF` – Notifications, tips, help text

**Grayscale**
- **Text Primary:** `#191919` (99% of body text)
- **Text Secondary:** `#4a4a4a` (labels, captions)
- **Text Tertiary:** `#999999` (disabled, hints)
- **Background:** `#f9f9f9` (page background, light surfaces)
- **Surface:** `#ffffff` (cards, containers, modals)
- **Border:** `#e0e0e0` (dividers, input borders)

### 2.2 Typography

**Sans-Serif (Primary) – Inter**
- Default: Inter, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Segoe UI, sans-serif
- Usage: All body text, labels, UI elements
- Font weights: 400 (regular), 500 (medium), 600 (semibold), 700 (bold)

**Display Font (Headlines)**
- Use **Inter 600** (semibold) for all H1, H2, H3 headings
- Letter-spacing: -0.03em to -0.02em (tighter on large text)
- Font sizes: H1 (54px), H2 (44px), H3 (22px) – use clamp() for responsiveness

**Monospace (Code & Data)**
- JetBrains Mono or system monospace
- Usage: Numbers, transaction IDs, invoice codes, code blocks

### 2.3 Iconography

**Icon Library:** Heroicons Pro (recommended) or Material Design Icons (fallback)
- Stroke weight: 1.5px for 24px icons
- Color: Inherit from context (orange for active, gray for secondary)
- Usage: Navigation, status indicators, actions

**Custom Icon Guidelines:**
- All custom icons must use 1.5px stroke weight
- No filled icons (use stroked/outline style only)
- 24×24px base size; scale with text
- Color consistency: orange for primary actions, gray for secondary

### 2.4 Logo Usage

**Ledgr Logo Mark**
- Primary mark: Dark "L" in rounded square, orange background (`#FF5C00`)
- Usage: Favicon, navigation bar, brand asset
- Minimum size: 22×22px
- Do NOT distort, skew, or change proportions

**Logo with Logotype**
- Logo + "Ledgr" text (Inter 600, 17px) with 9px gap
- Usage: Website header, marketing materials
- Minimum width: 140px

## 3. Tone & Voice

### 3.1 Tone of Voice

**Professional yet approachable** — We speak like a trusted advisor, not a bot.

| Attribute | Example |
|-----------|---------|
| **Clear, not jargon-heavy** | "Your FTA e-invoice is ready to submit" NOT "Your UBL/Peppol transaction artifact is queued for regulatory transmission" |
| **Confident, not pushy** | "Try Ledgr free for 30 days" NOT "Don't miss out! Sign up NOW!!!" |
| **Helpful, not condescending** | "Here's why your VAT return didn't match" NOT "You made an error" |
| **Human-first, not corporate** | "We're here when you need us (24/7)" NOT "World-class customer success solutions" |

### 3.2 Voice Guidelines

- **Use "you/your"** when addressing users (direct, personal)
- **Use "we/Ledgr"** when speaking as the company
- **Avoid:**
  - Exclamation points (unless genuinely excited)
  - ALL CAPS (except for emphasis on specific terms like "FREE")
  - Unnecessary buzzwords ("synergize," "leverage," "disrupt")
  - Gender-specific pronouns (use "they," "them" or rephrase)

### 3.3 Messaging Pillars

1. **Compliance Made Simple** – FTA compliance shouldn't require an accountant
2. **Time Reclaimed** – Spend 2 hours on compliance, not 80
3. **Built for UAE** – Native compliance, local language, local support
4. **AI That Works** – Automation you can trust, not a black box

## 5. Visual Communication Guidelines

### 5.1 Photography

**Style:** Authentic, documentary-style photography of real UAE businesses
- Diverse teams (men, women, various backgrounds)
- Actual work environments (not stock photo studios)
- Close-ups of screens, hands-on work
- Daylight, natural settings preferred

**Avoid:**
- Cheesy stock photos (smiling teams in suits)
- Generic corporate imagery
- Overly polished, artificial scenes

### 5.2 Illustrations

**Style:** Clean, minimalist line drawings with subtle color accents
- Orange as the primary illustration color
- Grayscale for secondary elements
- Stroke-based (not filled)
- Simple metaphors: graphs, documents, handshakes, automation icons

### 5.3 Data Visualization

- **Charts:** Simple bar/line charts with orange accent colors
- **Status Indicators:** Green (compliant), Red (issue), Amber (review needed)
- **Tables:** Light gray headers (`#f9f9f9`), white rows, orange row highlights on hover
- **Infographics:** Orange primary, grayscale supporting elements

## 7. What NOT to Do

- ❌ Change the orange color (it's our brand signature)
- ❌ Use drop-shadow on the logo (only the mark itself has subtle gradient)
- ❌ Distort or skew the logo proportions
- ❌ Use script or decorative fonts (stick to Inter + monospace)
- ❌ Overcomplicate diagrams (keep them simple, minimal)
- ❌ Use red and green together (not color-blind friendly; use orange + blue)
- ❌ Over-use exclamation marks (sounds unprofessional)
- ❌ Make vague claims ("world-class," "revolutionary") without proof
