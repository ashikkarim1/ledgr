5. **Reply-To:** Use support@ for customer inquiries

### Email Sequence Customization

Each email template can be customized in your ESP:

1. **Personalization:** Add `{{firstName}}` merge tags where names appear
2. **Tracking:** Enable link tracking to monitor which blog posts drive clicks
3. **A/B Testing:** Test subject lines (try alternate phrasings: "Is manual compliance costing you?" vs "The Real Cost...")
4. **Timing:** Experiment with send times (test 9am, 1pm, 6pm in recipient timezone)
5. **Segmentation:** Send different version to beta participants (emphasize "early access")

## Part 6: Content Themes & Key Messages

### Consistent Messaging Across All Channels

**CRITICAL CONSTRAINT:** Do NOT mention "20% or referral amounts or values" in any content.

Instead, focus on:
- **Speed:** "Fast close cycles," "Real-time visibility," "Accelerated delivery"
- **Quality:** "Fewer errors," "Consistent methodology," "Comprehensive audit trails"
- **Value:** "Clients get insights faster," "Teams focus on advisory work," "Competitive advantage"
- **Trust:** "AI-supervised, not replaced," "Human judgment built-in," "Transparent and documented"

### Brand Voice Guidelines

- **Tone:** Professional but approachable; confident but not arrogant
- **Length:** Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences), scannable lists
- **POV:** Speak to accounting firm leaders and senior accountants
- **Avoid:** Technical jargon, overpromising, price discussions
- **Emphasize:** Results, transparency, respect for professional judgment
