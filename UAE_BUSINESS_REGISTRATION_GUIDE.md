## 💼 PHASE 5: TAX & FINANCIAL COMPLIANCE

### Step 1: Determine Tax Status
**UAE Fintech Tax Treatment**:
- Free Zone Companies: Generally tax-exempt on JAFZA-earned income
- Onshore income: Subject to corporate tax (2023+)
- VAT applicability depends on service type

**Actions**:
- [ ] Consult with UAE tax advisor
- [ ] Determine if Ledgr services subject to VAT
- [ ] Ledgr accounting platform: Likely **no VAT** (financial service exemption)

### Step 2: VAT Registration (If Applicable)
**Threshold**: AED 375,000 annual revenue

**If exceeding threshold**:
1. [ ] Register with FTA (Federal Tax Authority)
2. [ ] Get VAT number
3. [ ] Issue VAT-compliant invoices
4. [ ] File monthly/quarterly VAT returns

### Step 3: Corporate Income Tax (2023+)
**UAE Corporate Tax**:
- Rate: 0% for companies earning less than AED 375,000
- Rate: 15% on profits above threshold

**Actions**:
- [ ] Monitor profitability
- [ ] Prepare for tax filing if profits exceed threshold
- [ ] Consider tax optimization strategies with accountant

### Step 4: Employer Obligations
**If hiring employees**:
- [ ] Register with Ministry of Human Resources
- [ ] Deduct employee contributions (Old Age Insurance: ~5.5%)
- [ ] Provide employee benefits per UAE Labor Law
- [ ] File monthly MOHRE reports
