## VISUAL DIRECTION & BRAND GUIDELINES

### Color Scheme
- **Primary Brand Color:** Orange `#FF5C00` — all CTA buttons, highlights, badges
- **Backgrounds:** Light gray/white (`#f9f9f9`, `#ffffff`)
- **Text:** Dark gray (`#191919` for primary, `#4a4a4a` for secondary)
- **Accent Lighting:** Subtle orange glow behind key UI elements

### Typography
- **Headlines:** Inter 600 (semibold), white or dark gray
- **Body:** Inter 400 (regular), dark gray
- **On-screen text:** Clear, readable, no more than 5 words per badge

### Pacing & Tone
- **Overall pace:** Professional but energetic (not corporate-boring)
- **Transitions:** Clean, modern (dissolve, subtle zoom, no spinning effects)
- **Music:** Modern electronic/ambient, uplifting but serious
- **Voiceover:** Male or female, confident tone, no rushed reading
- **Sound effects:** Subtle (light notifications, data processing sounds) — not cartoonish

### Key Visual Moments (Must-Include)

1. **Invoice Processing:** Show an invoice entering system → FTA validation → Green checkmark ✓
2. **Accuracy Metrics:** "99.9%" prominently displayed with live transaction examples
3. **Before/After Accountant:** Stressed vs. relaxed visual contrast
4. **Dashboard Clarity:** Clear, readable charts and KPIs (no blurry text)
5. **Arabic Support:** Brief moment showing bilingual interface option
6. **Speed:** Show multiple transactions processing simultaneously
