Common questions

About this flow.

Do you actually see my bank login?

No. The Open Finance link is brokered by Lean or Tarabut — both UAE-licensed under the CBUAE Open Finance framework. We receive read-only transaction data with your consent, and you can revoke the connection from your dashboard at any time.

What if I don't have 18 months of history?

We use whatever you have. For a newly incorporated entity, we ingest from the date of incorporation. If you have paper statements only, drag them into the inbox — the Capture agent OCRs them in the same pass.

Can I migrate from Xero or Zoho?

Yes. We import your chart of accounts, opening balances, customers and vendors. The migration typically runs in the background while you continue onboarding, and a human reviewer reconciles the cutover before you go live.

Who sees the classifications?

You, your dedicated chartered accountant, and the agent itself. No other party has read access. The reasoning trail is preserved with every classification and is exportable in CSV or JSON.
